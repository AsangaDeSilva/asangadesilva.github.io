Online Bus Ticket Reservation System

Online Bus Ticket Reservation System is a web-based platform that enables users to book bus tickets easily, securely, and conveniently from anywhere. In here my collaboration is timetable management. The system allows passengers to search for available bus routes, view schedules, select seats, and make payments online. It eliminates the need for physical ticket counters, reduces manual errors, and improves overall efficiency in managing bus transportation services. Designed for both customers and administrators, the system includes features such as real-time seat availability, user registration, booking history, and automated notifications for confirmation and reminders.

AI - Poweered Presentation Scheduler

AI-Powered Presentation Scheduler is an intelligent scheduling system designed to automatically organize and optimize presentation timetables using artificial intelligence. In here my part is Scheduling the Presentations. Ideal for academic institutions, conferences, or corporate events, this solution reduces manual effort, enhances transparency, and ensures fairness. Key features include real-time updates, drag-and-drop rescheduling, clash detection, and personalized notifications for all participants.

Classical Phone

I designed and modeled a vintage-style telephone using Autodesk Maya, focusing on accurate proportions and intricate detailing. The project involved high-poly modeling, UV unwrapping, and texturing to achieve a realistic look. I applied basic lighting and rendering techniques using Arnold Renderer to highlight the model’s retro aesthetics. This piece was created as part of my portfolio to demonstrate my 3D modeling skills, with potential use in games, animation, or product visualization.
